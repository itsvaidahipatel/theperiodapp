Data Management & Account Deletion

This page explains how to request permanent account and data deletion for PeriodCycle.AI.

Delete Data In-App (Instant)

To delete your account and associated health data directly in the app:

  1. Open the app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile > Account Actions > Delete Account.
  3. Confirm deletion when prompted. This action is permanent.

Web Request Option (If You Uninstalled)

If you no longer have the app installed, submit a deletion request by email and our support team will assist you.
